About Nefsk Feeh

Nefsk Feeh is a home-based food platform that brings you authentic Egyptian and Middle Eastern homemade meals. Our mission is to connect talented home chefs with people who want fresh, homemade food cooked with passion.

Our Vision

We aim to make homemade food accessible to everyone while supporting local home chefs by giving them a trusted platform to sell their delicious meals.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fresh homemade meals cooked daily
  • Trusted home chefs
  • Variety of cuisines and categories
  • Affordable prices
